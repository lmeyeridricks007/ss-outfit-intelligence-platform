# Vision

## Product vision

AI Outfit Intelligence Platform is SuitSupply's recommendation foundation for generating complete, context-aware outfits instead of isolated item suggestions. The platform should combine curated merchandising knowledge, compatibility rules, customer signals, and machine-learned ranking so every channel can present recommendations that feel stylistically coherent, commercially relevant, and personalized.

## Why this product should exist

SuitSupply already has strong product curation and style expertise, but that expertise is not consistently activated in a data-driven way across ecommerce, clienteling, and marketing surfaces. Customers often know the anchor item they want, such as a suit or jacket, but need help building the full look around it. Existing recommendation patterns based on similarity, popularity, or co-occurrence do not solve that styling problem well enough.

This product should exist to turn SuitSupply's style point of view into a reusable platform capability that can:
- help customers complete outfits with confidence
- increase basket size and conversion by recommending compatible complementary items
- adapt recommendations to context such as season, weather, occasion, and location
- personalize suggestions using customer behavior, purchase history, and profile signals
- give merchandisers and stylists control over the recommendation logic without losing AI-driven optimization

## Long-term ambition

Over time, the platform should become the canonical recommendation layer for all major customer and internal channels:
- online store surfaces such as PDP, cart, homepage, and look-builder experiences
- email and campaign personalization
- in-store stylist and clienteling workflows
- future mobile, API-driven, and partner surfaces

The long-term ambition is to move from isolated recommendations to a full outfit intelligence system that can:
- understand product compatibility across RTW and CM assortments
- compose multiple looks for a given intent or occasion
- balance business rules, inventory, customer preference, and contextual relevance
- provide explainable recommendation controls for merchandising and optimization teams

## Differentiators

This product should differentiate SuitSupply from standard ecommerce recommendation systems through:
- complete-look recommendation logic rather than item similarity alone
- explicit styling intelligence based on compatibility, occasion, and brand curation
- support for both Ready-to-Wear and Custom Made journeys
- multi-channel delivery across digital, marketing, and in-store workflows
- a blended recommendation model that supports curated, rule-based, and AI-ranked outputs
- strong governance so merchandising teams can shape outcomes while analytics teams measure performance

## Intended market and user impact

For customers, the platform should reduce outfit-building friction and make style discovery feel more confident, relevant, and premium.

For internal teams, it should create a shared recommendation capability that improves consistency across channels, reduces manual curation overhead, and enables continuous optimization.

## Vision statement

SuitSupply should deliver the right complete look for the customer, occasion, and context, using a governed recommendation platform that blends brand curation, customer understanding, and AI ranking across every channel.
