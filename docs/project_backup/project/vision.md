# AI Outfit Intelligence Platform Vision

## Purpose
The AI Outfit Intelligence Platform is a style intelligence system for SuitSupply that recommends complete looks, contextual next-best products, and merchandising-guided outfit combinations across digital and assisted selling channels. The platform is designed to move beyond single-item recommendation toward outfit-level decision support.

## Vision Statement
Deliver the right complete look, for the right customer, in the right context, through the right channel, with enough merchandising control and explainability that business teams trust it and customers act on it.

## North Star
Transform recommendation from "people also bought" into "this is the full outfit this customer should see next."

## Strategic Outcomes
- Increase conversion by showing coherent, occasion-aware looks instead of disconnected products.
- Increase basket size through intelligent cross-sell, upsell, and bundle recommendations.
- Improve customer lifetime value by learning evolving style preferences across purchases and channels.
- Enable merchandising teams to guide, approve, and override AI behavior instead of being bypassed by it.
- Create a reusable recommendation foundation for web, email, in-store clienteling, and future personalization surfaces.

## Core Recommendation Promise
The platform must answer high-value style questions such as:
- What should this customer wear to a wedding, interview, or summer event?
- What shirt, shoe, knitwear, and accessories complete this suit?
- What should this customer buy next based on prior purchases and stated interests?
- What curated look should be shown on this product detail page in this region, season, and weather condition?

## Product Principles
1. Outfit first, item second.
2. Personalization must respect merchandising intent.
3. Recommendations must be channel aware and context aware.
4. RTW and CM journeys must share intelligence but support different constraints.
5. Every recommendation should be measurable, governable, and explainable.

## Key Differentiators
- Outfit graph combining products, looks, style rules, and occasion signals.
- Hybrid recommendation strategy using curated looks, explicit rules, and AI ranking.
- Shared intelligence across online store, email, clienteling, and future on-site personalization.
- Support for both Ready-to-Wear and Custom Made journeys.
- Human-in-the-loop approval model for merchandising-sensitive outputs.

## Long-Term Vision
- Conversational styling assistance.
- Occasion and trip packing recommendations.
- Guided wardrobe building over multiple seasons.
- Associate-assisted styling in-store.
- Generative look ideation for merchandising teams.
